FAQs

1. What is Curefind?
Curefind is a medical reminder platform designed to help users keep track of their medication schedules with timely notifications and reminders.

2. How do I set up a medical reminder?
Once you sign up, you can easily create reminders by entering your medication details, dosage, and frequency. The platform will send you notifications based on your preferences.

3. Is Curefind free to use?
Yes, Curefind offers free services for basic reminders. However, we may introduce premium features in the future for advanced functionalities.

4. Can I edit or delete my reminders? Absolutely! You can edit or delete any reminder through the user dashboard at any time.

5. How secure is my data? We take your privacy seriously and employ strict security measures to protect your data. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more details.

6. What happens if I miss a reminder?
Curefind will send you a notification to remid you of your medication. However, you are responsiblle for ensuring that you take your medication as prescribed
